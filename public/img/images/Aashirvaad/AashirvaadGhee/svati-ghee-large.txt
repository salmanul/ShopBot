Aashirvaad Svasti Ghee is the first product from ITC's dairy portfolio. In India, different kinds of ghee exist, catering to the regional taste palates. ITC's Aashirvaad Svasti Ghee is an outcome of R&D at the ITC Life Sciences and Technology Centre, Bengaluru, which studied these different regional profiles of ghee, to craft out superior regional variants for the consumers.
